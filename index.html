Features

Three tools every homeowner needs

HomeBase combines AI-powered diagnostics, a personalized home profile, and smart maintenance tracking — all in one app.

Smart Project Diagnosis

Describe what's going on — in plain English. HomeBase asks the right follow-up questions, then gives you a clear plan: step-by-step DIY guidance or a pro referral with a script for what to tell the contractor.

Your Home's Digital Brain

Save your home's details — zip code, age, systems, equipment model numbers — and HomeBase uses it all to give smarter, more specific advice every time you start a project.

Maintenance on Autopilot

Changing filters, cleaning gutters, flushing the water heater — the stuff you always forget. HomeBase sends personalized reminders based on your home so nothing falls through the cracks.

How It Works

From "what is that?" to "I fixed it"

No Googling, no doom-scrolling through forum threads. Just describe what's going on and HomeBase handles the rest.

1

Describe what's happening

Type it out or snap a photo. Use whatever words you want — HomeBase speaks homeowner, not contractor.

2

Answer a few smart questions

HomeBase narrows down your situation with targeted follow-ups — like a knowledgeable friend who actually knows what they're talking about.

3

Get your plan

DIY? You'll get a step-by-step walkthrough with product recommendations. Need a pro? You'll get a contractor script and cost estimate — no awkward phone calls.

AI That's Honest

It'll tell you when not to do it yourself

Most apps want you to buy more stuff. HomeBase tells you the truth — even when the answer is "hire someone."

DIY — You've Got This

Mount a TV? No sweat.

HomeBase walks you through the project, recommends the exact mount to buy, and gives you step-by-step instructions tailored to your TV and wall type.

Stop — Call a Professional

Aluminum wiring? Hard no.

HomeBase flags safety risks before you touch anything. It explains why, gives you a cost estimate, and tells you exactly what to say when you call an electrician.
