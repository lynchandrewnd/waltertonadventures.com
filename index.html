Meet Walter, our Chief Morale Officer

About Walterton Adventures LLC

We build tools that make homeownership less intimidating. Based in Colorado, Walterton Adventures is focused on using AI to help people take better care of their homes.

Our mission

Buying your first home is exciting — and then something breaks. Walterton Adventures was founded to close the knowledge gap between new homeowners and the DIY skills they need. We believe everyone deserves clear, trustworthy guidance that helps them decide what to tackle themselves and when to call a professional.

Why HomeBase

HomeBase is our first product: an iOS app that pairs AI-powered project guidance with a personalized home profile. Instead of sifting through generic YouTube videos and forum threads, homeowners get step-by-step advice tailored to their specific home, equipment, and skill level — all in one place.

Our approach

Safety comes first. HomeBase is designed to recognize when a project exceeds safe DIY territory — like anything involving a gas line, main electrical panel, or structural load — and recommends a licensed professional instead. We'd rather be honest about limitations than put someone at risk.
